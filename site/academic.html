A Chinese poem

Chinese Poem

酷仙之冬季度海

云舟泊落阳, 浪梯踏天堂.
咏月弹烟丝, 斟风醉雪洋.

This poem reflects a person's optimistic spirit for life and boundless creativity even when facing hardship. His imaginations of cloud reflections as boats berthing the setting sun, and waves forming stairs to the heavens, are far different than the reality of his journey of crossing a winter sea. Although stucked with nothing, he can still sing about the moon, play strings formed by vapor and pour wind as wine. I wrote this to remind myself of keeping a good state of mind when facing difficult research problems.
